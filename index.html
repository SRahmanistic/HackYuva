ABOUT

Who are we?

LearnYuva is an Ed-tech start-up. The main motive of LearnYuva is to provide courses/education in an efficient method. Hackathon is a platform where we bring innovative minds to solve real-time problems. Nowadays, hackathons are in demand and many colleges are making it compulsory for their students to participate in hackathons. To solve their problem, we are planning to conduct a hackathon from 30th October to 1st November, where both individual and team participation (maximum team size is 4) will be allowed.

Schedule



  • October 25

    Registration Closes

    Registration of teams for the Hackathon ends. No more teams can register for HackYuva after 25th.

  • October 26

    Problem Statement is released.

    The problem statement for the Hackathon is released. Participating teams must think of a solution on the given problem and come up with a solution.

  • October 30

    Opening Ceremony
    6 PM

    Guest lecture to start the Hackathon with the introduction of the Judges. Rules of the Hackathon are declared by the guest. Participating teams should submit their ideas through mails in the form of a power point presentation.

  • HackYuva Begins

    October 30

    The 36 hour hackathon starts
    7:00 PM

    How about giving some functionality to your HTML elements ? You may want your elements to rotate, to swing or to dance. Javascript is the thing that makes it possible. This session will teach you the basics as well as will show you some of the important aspects of Javascript in website development.
    [Hands On Evening Session(4PM - 5PM)]

  • October 31

    24 hours into Hackathon

    Every team must submit a prototype of the work they've done in the past 24 hours. Some fun activity like musical night might be organised. 7:00 PM

  • November 1

    Submission & Presentation

    By 9:00 AM, teams must submit their work. 8 teams will be shortlisted by the judges to present their work. Presentation will start from 1:00 PM, each team has a time limit of 30 minutes for presentation.

  • Hackathon
    Ends!

    November 1
    7:00 PM

    Closing Ceremony

    Results of the Hackathon will be declared, prizes and certificates will be distributed.
